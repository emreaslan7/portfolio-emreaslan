# Introduction

Soulbound tokens were announced in May 2022 with the whitepaper titled "[Decentralized Society: Finding Web3 Soul](https://researchhub-paper-prod.s3.amazonaws.com/uploads/papers/2022/05/17/SSRN-id4105763.pdf?AWSAccessKeyId=AKIA3RZN3OVNPLBMN3JX&Signature=5eW5Zix0MY3wgI3oJGV0ztrvQBQ%3D&Expires=1666341393)" by Ethereum co-founder Vitalik Buterin, economist Puja Ohlhaver, and sociologist E. Glen Weyl.

The whitepaper lays out the foundation for a fully decentralized society (DeSoc) managed by its users, and how Soulbound tokens (SBTs) can function as credentials.

Soulbound token (SBT), is defined as a concept "_the soul_" of crypto wallets.

<div align="center">

![](https://cdn.hashnode.com/res/hashnode/image/upload/v1666446672806/UEYXv32NN.png)

</div>

SBT token can connect only to a wallet and is like non-transferrable NFT tokens. NFTs are unique; however, are able to transferable whereas SBTs can not be transferred to another wallet.

I can explain to the purpose of being defined as a soul with a simple example. If we can think that the user's wallet is the body and the soul-bound token is the soul, we can understand that they are inseparable duos.

Once soulbound tokens are publicly assigned to a wallet, the wallet will now forever own that NFT. These tokens are non-saleable, non-transferable and non-leaseable tokens.

In short, SoulBound Token represents the key to unlocking access to a group that needs members to prove their identity.

Soulbound tokens are as Vitalik Buterin said indispensable idea especially for DAOs.

# How Soulbound Tokens Work?

Every crypto wallet is defined as a "body" that can store soulbound token. Soul wallets are responsible for verifying SBTs' data before publishing it for storage, owing to SBTs are non-transferable. In short, SBTs are connected to wallets forever. Thus, anyone can looking for your soulbound tokens for different purposes.
